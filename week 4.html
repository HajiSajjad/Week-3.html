WHO WE ARE
NVO is providing services in the domain of Student and Women empowerment

About Organization
New Village Educational, Social, Developmental Organization
NVESDO is acronym of New Village Education, Social, Developmental Organization which is non- Governmental, non- profitable humanitarian and charity-based organization in Quetta city Baluchistan, Pakistan.
BBW was formed in 2017 to promote and help all vulnerable groups such as women and girls, men and boys, person with disabilities and poor students.
  • All vulnerable must be treated with respect, prestige, and dignity.
  • Freedom to choose is the basic right of all.
  • All vulnerable must be able to enjoy full mobility and accessibility to have access to the community.
